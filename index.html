WHY LEARN RUBY ON RAILS?

It's one of the most common platforms for building web apps, because it's one of the fastest ways to build them from scratch.

Ruby on Rails is also one of the most sought-after computer languages by employers. We'll connect you with local employers who are hiring Rails engineers, but you are under no pressure to work for these companies.

WHY BERLIN?

Berlin is one of the hottest start-up cities in Europe. You'll be at startup ground zero here so you'll be able to connect with potential employers and enjoy the tech culture.

Also, living in Berlin is really cheap compared to other major American and European cities, while you have a high standard of living.

FOR WHOM IS THIS NOT?

Learning by doing is the best way to learn how to code. If you're not willing to work hard and hands-on, this boot camp is not for you.

You'll be living and breathing Ruby on Rails every day of the week with our experienced coaches, so if you're can't take constructive criticism, this is not for you.

FOR WHOM IS THIS?

We're looking for bright, motivated students who want to improve their career. We're looking for dedicated problem solvers who want to build awesome things.
